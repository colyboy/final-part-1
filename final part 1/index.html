COAST AND CANYON WILDLIFE REHABILITATION


Coast & Canyon Wildlife Rehabilitation is a volunteer run organization that helps injured and orphaned wildlife of the Los Angeles area, primarily baby squirrels. Last year our team of volunteers cared for over 1,100 orphaned, injured, or displaced wild animals in Los Angeles County. We pride ourselves on never turning away an animal in need, and never letting the cost of an animal's care determine its future.
